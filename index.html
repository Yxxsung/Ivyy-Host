Fun and Rich in Lore

Hades a dungeon crawler rpg game created by SuperGiant Games in 2020. This game is action packed, full of intense and intriguing lore,
and can be played on many platforms such as MacOS, Nintendo Switch, and Windows. The game features many
different enemies on the three levels of the underworld, each containing many individual levels for the player to complete to move on.
Having won the 2018 Hugo Award for best game, this unique and addicting work of art is beyond worth its price tag.
